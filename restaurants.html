The city's culinary delights are as diverse as its people, reflecting a fusion of traditional Gujarati flavours and influences from other parts of India. Exploring the bustling streets of Ahmedabad, one will encounter an array of lip-smacking street food offerings that tantalise the taste buds and leave an everlasting impression.
